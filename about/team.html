Meet The Team

We're Team Rose Motorsports

Hello and welcome to our website! We're TRM, made of Rose-Hulman students of various majors interested in cars and racing. We're based out of the Rose-Hulman BIC.

Team Rose Motorsports at the PRI Trade Show in 2023

Mission Statement

Team Rose Motorsports' purpose is to provide an organized group of assets in the form of experience, tools, knowledge, facilities, and events whereby members can apply engineering skills developed in the classroom to develop and expand their passion for motorsports. By working on and driving team owned project cars in sanctioned competitions in the surrounding area, members can build their knowlege of automobiles and high performance driving, which can be applied to their careers in the future.
