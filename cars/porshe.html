The Porshe

Facts and Info

The Porshe also exists, although it hasn't been fully assembled for some time. All I know is that it's a 1974 Porsche 914 and is powered by a Mazda Series 5 (S5) 13B (turbo 2) out of an FC RX7. The rollcage makes it quite difficult to get into and the clutch feels weird.
