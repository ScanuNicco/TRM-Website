The New Mustang

Facts and Info

The New Mustang is our 1994 Cobra, which is currently engineless. We're planning to rebuild a Ford 302 that used to be in the Old Mustang to remedy this issue. While it will eventually become out second autocross car, we still have to put in a lot of work before it is race-ready!
