The RX-7

Facts and Info

Our 7 started life in 1985 as a silver Mazda RX-7 GSL-SE. Team Rose Motorsports acquired her in the fall of 2001 with plans for an all-out auto-crosser. Modifications included new sway bars, coilovers, intake and exhaust, ITBs, an LSD, and a completely stripped interior for maximum weight savings. The 7 was replaced with the BMW in early 2011.
