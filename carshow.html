Car Show

2024 Fall Car Show

The bi-annual TRM car show is a one of the main ways we connect to our surrounding communities. This year's car show will be held at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology on September 28th, 2024. This one will be on the smaller side, so if you have an interesting vehicle please consider registering! Free pizza will be available.
